Brand Leadership Africa

100-Day Launch Blueprint

Mission & Vision

  • Become the WPP of Africa
  • Amplify African brands globally
  • Create a pan-African creative powerhouse

Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1–30)

  • Clarify vision, mission, and positioning
  • Conduct market and consumer research
  • Audit internal capabilities
  • Create foundational brand assets and pitch decks

Phase 2: Activation (Days 31–70)

  • Acquire flagship clients and deliver pilot campaigns
  • Recruit top creative, digital, and strategy talent
  • Deploy technology and collaboration tools
  • Build case studies and proof points

Phase 3: Acceleration (Days 71–100)

  • Establish strategic partnerships
  • Launch thought leadership & PR campaigns
  • Implement agency systems and culture
  • Plan regional expansion and next wave clients

Metrics for Success (First 100 Days)

  • Flagship clients acquired: 1–2
  • Case studies / proof points: 2–3
  • Key talent onboarded: 5–10
  • Strategic partnerships initiated: 2–3
  • Media coverage / thought leadership mentions: 3–5

Next Steps

  • Finalize 100-day action plan with leadership
  • Assign owners to each phase
  • Prepare pitch materials for clients and investors
  • Launch first pilot campaign by Day 45
